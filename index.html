Welcome to the first annual convention for Dr. Seuss! This convention is dedicated to the literary works of Dr. Seuss. There are many different booths throughout the convention that have different works being discussed.

When:

Monday, February 16th @ 8:00 am

Where:

Rochester Institute of Technology!

8:00 am
Doors Open and Sign in

Description Brief

9:00 am
Introduction Speech

Kicking off this convention is a speech from the people who brought you the 2000 movie "How the Grinch Stole Christmas", Jeffrey Price and Peter S. Seaman!

10:00 am
Open Browsing

Feel free to walk around the convention looking at the literary works of Dr. Seuss.

12:00 pm
Lunch

Use the meal coupon that was included in your packet when you signed in at any of the locations on RIT Campus.

1:00 pm
Open Browsing

Continue to walk around to the different areas of the convention looking at literary works of Dr. Seuss.

4:00 pm
Closing Remarks

As this convention goes to a end, come and listen to our closing remarks about Dr. Seuss's literary works and how they have affect us as children and for our future children of the world.

6:00 pm
Doors Close

Come back next year for more Dr. Seuss!
